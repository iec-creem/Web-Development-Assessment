A hauntingly beautiful journey through a vast, hand-drawn underground kingdom.

Hollow Knight, released in 2017, invites players into the vast, interconnected world of Hallownest, a hand-drawn underground kingdom filled with mystery, challenges, and secrets to uncover. Its hauntingly beautiful atmosphere is elevated by Christopher Larkin's orchestral score, which captures the quiet beauty of exploration, the tension of boss battles, and the lingering melancholy that defines the game's tone.

City of Tears

A beautiful yet somber piano and string composition that mirrors the endless rain of the City of Tears. It plays as you explore this sprawling, melancholy location deep within Hallownest. The music captures the city's history, grandeur, and quiet sadness.

Hornet

A tense, energetic orchestral track that matches the agility and ferocity of Hornet's movements. It is heard during both encounters with Hornet, creating a heightened sense of urgency. The quick rhythms and sharp melodies make each battle feel like a duel of skill.

Greenpath

A light, flowing orchestral piece with hints of mystery, reflecting the lush greenery and hidden dangers of Greenpath. It plays as soon as you enter this vibrant biome, mixing beauty with a sense of the unknown. The melody invites exploration while reminding you that danger lurks nearby.
