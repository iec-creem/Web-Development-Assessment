Game Music Archive

Relive the game through its music

Undertale

Undertale is a unique indie RPG where you choose whether to fight, spare, or befriend your enemies. Every decision shapes the story, leading to multiple endings and surprising twists. Its heartfelt characters, humor, and unforgettable soundtrack have made it a modern classic.

Pokemon

The Pokémon series invites players to explore vibrant worlds, catch and train a wide variety of creatures, and battle to become a Pokémon Champion. Across its many generations, it has combined exploration, strategy, and collection in a way that encourages friendship, adventure, and discovery. From nostalgic chiptune melodies to fully orchestrated themes, Pokémon's music has remained an iconic part of the experience for fans of all ages.

Hollow Knight

Hollow Knight is a beautifully hand-drawn action-adventure set in the mysterious underground kingdom of Hallownest. Players explore vast interconnected areas, face challenging enemies, and uncover hidden lore. The game's hauntingly atmospheric soundtrack perfectly complements its rich and immersive world.
