A unique RPG where every action has consequences, shaping the world and its characters.

Undertale is a one-of-a-kind RPG where every choice you make has lasting consequences, influencing the story, characters, and even the battles themselves. Released in 2015, it gained instant acclaim for its unique storytelling and replayability. Its soundtrack, composed entirely by Toby Fox, shifts seamlessly between lighthearted chiptunes, intense boss themes, and emotional melodies, creating a deeply personal and memorable experience for each player.

Fallen Down

A gentle, melancholic piano piece that radiates warmth and sadness in equal measure. It plays during tender story moments, most notably when meeting Toriel in the Ruins. Its soft melody leaves a lasting emotional imprint on the player.

Megalovania

A fast-paced, high-energy battle theme that's become one of the most recognizable tracks in gaming. It plays during the infamous fight against Sans in the game's Genocide Route, matching the intensity and relentlessness of the battle. The sharp chiptune melodies and driving beat make it a true adrenaline rush.

Hopes and Dreams

An epic, emotionally charged track that blends determination and hope into a climactic final battle theme. It is heard during the fight against Asriel Dreemurr, marking one of the game's most dramatic moments. The soaring melodies capture the urgency and weight of saving the world.
